monroecountypa.gov → Tax Claim → Delinquent List (updated quarterly)
REGISTER OF WILLS — Probate
monroecountypa.gov → Register of Wills → New estate filings
SHERIFF SALES — Pre-Foreclosure
monroecountypa.gov → Sheriff → Sheriff Sales
UJS PORTAL — Divorce + Foreclosure
ujsportal.pacourts.us → Case Search → Monroe County → Domestic Relations (divorce) or Civil / mortgage foreclosure
FLEX MLS — Expired + Price Drops
Flex MLS → Monroe County, SFR, Status=Expired, last 30–60 days. Also: Active with 2+ price reductions.
PERMITS ⭐ — Stroud / Pocono / Hamilton Townships
stroudtownship.com · poconotownship.org · hamiltontownship.org → Building Dept → New permits (kitchen, bath, addition)
