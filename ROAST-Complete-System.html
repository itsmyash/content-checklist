You are a real estate policy analyst. I am a licensed realtor in Pennsylvania (PA License #RS326383) working in the Pocono Mountains / Monroe County short-term rental investor market. I also work Dominican Republic real estate. Research and report on ALL of the following for the week of [INSERT CURRENT DATE]: FEDERAL LEVEL: - Any new or proposed federal real estate legislation introduced or advanced this week - FEMA flood zone policy updates, National Flood Insurance Program news - Federal housing policy changes affecting investors or STR operators - IRS or Treasury updates affecting real estate investors (depreciation, 1031 exchanges, short-term rental tax classification) - NAR policy updates or legal developments PENNSYLVANIA STATE LEVEL: - Pennsylvania Association of Realtors (PAR) legislative updates — track HB 818, SB 803, SB 815, HB 214, HB 986, HB 1103 and any new bills introduced - Pennsylvania housing market policy changes - Pennsylvania STR legislation or proposed regulations - Pennsylvania transfer tax, mortgage, or financing law changes MONROE COUNTY & POCONO MOUNTAINS LOCAL: - Monroe County zoning or STR ordinance updates - Township-level STR regulation changes — specifically: Barrett Township, Smithfield Township, Hamilton Township, Paradise Township, Pocono Township, Jackson Township - Any HOA regulation changes in major Pocono developments - Local government actions affecting real estate or STR operations - Monroe County court decisions affecting property rights or STR operations FORMAT AS: 1.
