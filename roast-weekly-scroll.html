Pocono Township: pricing power is holding but competitive set is growing 47.7%. You need a property that wins on differentiation, not just location. SECTION 3 (6:00–9:00) — What most realtors won't tell you If a seller is showing you peak-season nightly rates without 12-24 months of actual payout statements, that's not a data point — that's a hope. Ask for the statements. Re-underwrite to conservative occupancy. And remember: a 10-point occupancy drop requires a 25% ADR increase just to break even.
